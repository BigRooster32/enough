Privacy Policy

Enough is a free wellness app. This local build saves profile details, journal entries, moods, body notes, gratitude posts, purpose steps, and worship favorites on the user's device using browser storage.

No subscription is required. Do not enter emergency, medical, legal, or financial information into the app. Enough is for wellness reflection and does not replace professional care.

For a public App Store or Google Play launch, update this policy with your legal developer name, contact email, hosting provider, analytics tools, data retention policy, and account deletion process.
